HeroesDonate

2019

[Live]

HeroesDonate was my capstone project for my undergraduate degree. It was a web app with the intentions of making a digital donation website to connect donors to organizations. Donors would donate electronically giftable items, like Steam Keys, to organizations that had need for them. Or an organization could ask for donations and donors could fufill it. The website was akin to DonorsChoose but for ebooks, video games, and giftable subscriptions.

What made HeroesDonate different was that there was a public ledger to ensure that the donated goods were actually donated and not taken. The plan was to use a blockchain to verify transactions and publicly announce them without compromising the donation on the public ledger.

Then I used IPFS(InterPlanetary File System) hosted on a group of private nodes in Google Cloud to host the donation. The donations were hashed and the IPFS key was saved to my database. If you would like to see more about the project you can download my final proposal right here.
